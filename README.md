## Overview

Players must navigate the game that has platforms and enemies while collecting all required coins before reaching the finish area.

---

## Gameplay

The objective is to collect every coin in the level and then reach the finish area.

Players must:

1. Navigate across platforms.
2. Avoid enemies and hazards.
3. Collect all available coins.
4. Reach the goal area after collecting all coins.

If the player touches an enemy, the level automatically restarts.
If the player does not collect all coins, they will not pass the level.

---

## Controls

| Action | Controls |
|----------|----------|
| Move Left | A or Left Arrow |
| Move Right | D or Right Arrow |
| Jump | Spacebar, W, or Up Arrow |
| Double Jump | Press jump again while airborne |

---

## Features

### Player Movement

- Horizontal movement
- Ground detection
- Smooth jumping mechanics
- Double jump functionality

### Collectible System

- Players must collect every coin.
- Coin count updates during gameplay.
- Progress is tracked until all collectibles are obtained.

### Enemy System

- Contact with an enemy results in losing.
- The level automatically restarts when the player is defeated.

### Win Condition

- All coins have been collected.
- The player reaches the goal area.

### Lose Condition

The player loses if:

- They collide with an enemy.
- They fall into the water

When this occurs:

- The level automatically restarts.
- The progress will reset.

1. Start the game.
2. Move through the level using the keyboard.
3. Collect all coins.
4. Avoid enemies.
5. Reach the finish area after collecting every coin.
6. Complete the level to win.
